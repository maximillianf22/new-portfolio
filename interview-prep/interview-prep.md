## üéØ TIPS FOR 30-MINUTE INTERVIEWS

1. **Be concise** - Get to the point quickly
2. **Have 2-3 strong examples ready** - Use STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
3. **Ask smart questions** - Shows you're engaged
4. **Show enthusiasm** - But stay professional
5. **Listen carefully** - Don't interrupt
6. **It's okay to pause** - Better than rushing
7. **Speak slowly and clearly** - Especially if English isn't your first language
